ABOUT US

We Are Aniradichita

Creative, passionate, flexible, and personable — we deliver what you exactly want and then some. Smile your way through this journey of life!

Who We Are

Welcome to Aniradichita Theatre and Films Association, where innovation meets cultural art forms to deliver unparalleled solutions. As a distinguished not-for-profit Section 8 licensed company, we proudly stand among the top 50 startups of 2021 in the 3M-CII Young Innovators Challenge Awards.

As the pioneers of PAaaS — Performing Arts As A Service, we provide holistic services in marketing, training and development, CSR, events, and education, utilizing cultural art forms like drama, music, dance, blended with films and video creation.

We extend our services from ants to elephants — catering to businesses of all sizes, from small enterprises to large corporations.

Our Vision

Aniradichita aims to integrate cultural arts with business innovation, redefine the performing arts, and promote sustainability. We empower businesses of all sizes to utilize cultural arts, inspiring positive change and community connection.

Our Commitment

Excellence in Innovation: Continuously pushing creative boundaries, infusing cultural art forms into our services.

Client-Centric Approach: Your success is our priority — tailoring solutions to your unique business goals.

Affordability & Accessibility: Transformative power of cultural arts accessible to businesses of every size.

Sustainable Development: CSR projects and environmentally conscious practices for positive community impact.

Founder

Aniket Parimal Pandya — Engineer by Education · Artist by Passion · Entrepreneur by Profession. Associate Director of Gujarati film Safalta 0 Km on Amazon Prime. His performing arts modules were launched by the Union Minister of Skill Development Shri Rajeev Chandrashekhar.

WHAT WE DO

Services for Corporates & Businesses

Four powerful service verticals rooted in Performing Arts As A Service — from branding to CSR, training to events.

📣

Pravartan

Because Branding needs creativity
Pravartan: where creativity meets strategy, and ideas take flight. From compelling corporate films to flash mobs and creative marketing campaigns — theatrical branding, on-ground marketing, and live branding experiences designed to elevate your brand presence and leave an indelible mark on your audience's minds.
🎓

Aakhyan

Train, Transform, Triumph Experiencing Art
Aakhyan replaces traditional textbooks with real-world performing arts experiences. From team-building games to immersive business case studies, our experiential learning initiatives empower your teams to navigate daily challenges with confidence — a new era of human development through art.
🌱

Aamarsh

Crafting Impactful Narratives for Social Change
Aamarsh redefines corporate social responsibility through experiential and transformative performing arts projects. We conduct need assessment surveys and execute turnkey CSR projects — ensuring your corporate responsibility aligns with authentic community needs and creates a ripple effect of positive change.
🎪

Abhisarg

Transforming Events into Unforgettable Experiences
Abhisarg transforms events into captivating spectacles. From concept to execution — coordinating artists, managing performances, infusing theatrical magic. Whether a corporate gathering, product launch, or cultural extravaganza, Abhisarg delivers precision, creativity, and immersive experiences that make every event truly unforgettable.
